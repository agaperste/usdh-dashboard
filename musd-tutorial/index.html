TLDR

The 30-second version

1

mUSD wraps M0's M token

Created by wrapping M tokens through M0 Protocol -- 5+ onchain transactions instead of one direct mint.

2

Anyone can wrap/unwrap

Third parties independently wrap M into mUSD. Shows up onchain, doesn't exist in Bridge's ledger.

3

Cross-chain bridging creates phantom activity

Relay.link moves mUSD between chains via burn-on-source, mint-on-destination. $3-25M/day in gross activity that nets to ~$0.

4

Yield accrual inflates onchain supply

~$2K/day in new mUSD minted from M0's earning rate. Ledger doesn't track it -- ~$635-645K cumulative gap.

5

We whitelist Bridge wallets to reconcile

Only count mints/burns involving Bridge's three wallets. Everything else is automatically excluded.

Superstate USTB (yield-bearing) and USDC

Minters

Approved entities (like Bridge) that pledge collateral and mint M tokens. Bridge's minter: 0xCD1394d24e1E404F9eB3609F872B0736bEcb9d74.

Swap Facility

M0 contract that converts between M and mUSD. wrap() deposits M, mints mUSD. unwrap() burns mUSD, returns M. 1:1 conversion.

Earner Rate

M tokens accrue yield continuously. When wrapped as mUSD, yield accumulates in the WrappedMToken contract and is periodically claimed, minting new mUSD.

Mint Ratio

Bridge mints with a ratio of 0.99 -- for every $1 of collateral, 0.99 M tokens are minted. Provides a buffer.

Takeaway

Why the Numbers Don't Match

Two noise sources dominate mUSD's onchain data

🚨

#1: Cross-Chain Bridging (99%+ of noise)

Relay.link moves mUSD between Ethereum, Linea, and BSC by burning on source and minting on destination. On Feb 18, this generated $24.5M in gross mints and $24.5M in gross burns -- zero net impact on supply.

The Double-Counting Problem

Bridging $1M mUSD from Ethereum to Linea:

EthereumLineaAggregate
Mints$0+$1M+$1M
Burns-$1M$0-$1M
Net-$1M+$1M$0

Dozens of these per day at varying amounts = $24.5M gross mints + $24.5M gross burns on Feb 18. Supply unchanged.

#2: Yield Accrual (~$2K/day)

M0's earning rate continuously generates new mUSD minted from the zero address to 0x4177...5fbf. The ledger doesn't track this, creating a cumulative ~$635-645K supply gap. This is why mUSD's reserve ratio shows ~1.03.
